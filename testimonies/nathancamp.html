Nathan Camp

I was raised in a Christian home, and we faithfully attended Baptist churches all my life. After a weak revival on Feb 1st, 1981, at 6 years old, I was convicted of my sins. I went outside by our basketball court, knelt, and asked Jesus to forgive my sins, come into my heart, and save me.

He did! I have been faithfully serving the Lord in Baptist churches since.
