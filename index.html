Not Ashamed

Romans 1:16 is a powerful verse that reminds us of the importance of sharing the gospel of Christ with others. As believers, we should not be ashamed of the message of salvation, but rather, we should boldly proclaim it to the world around us. And that is exactly what we do here at Testimonies.

At Testimonies, we are passionate about sharing our stories with others. Our goal is to inspire and encourage those who may be going through tough times, and to offer hope and healing through the love of Jesus Christ. By sharing our experiences and how God has worked in our lives, we hope to show others that they are not alone in their struggles.

How to get to Heaven?

One of the most common misconceptions among both Christians and non-believers is the idea that being a good person is sufficient to get to heaven. This notion ignores the fundamental need for Jesus Christ's sacrifice on the cross for the sins of the world. It shows a lack of understanding of what God considers "good" and the absolute sinlessness required for holiness.

The Bible, which is God's inspired Word, has much to say about humanity's so-called "goodness." According to the Bible, God is not just good, but holy. God's standard for getting into heaven is not merely being good, but rather complete freedom from sin. Since humans can never be holy enough to get to heaven, God provided a way through justification for them to be credited with the righteousness of Jesus Christ.

The ABC guide to getting saved is a simple, yet effective way to understand how to receive salvation.

  • A - Admit that you are a sinner. The Bible states in Romans 3:23, "For all have sinned, and come short of the glory of God."
  • B - Believe that Jesus Christ came to this earth and died for your sins. John 3:16 emphasizes this point, "For God so loved the world, that he gave his only begotten Son, that whosoever believeth in him should not perish, but have everlasting life."
  • C - Call upon the Lord to save you from your sins. Romans 10:13 states: "For whosoever shall call upon the name of the Lord shall be saved."

If you would like to read about how to get saved in more detail, you can visit the How to Get Saved section of the OBBC website. This page provides a comprehensive guide on how to receive salvation through Jesus Christ. It explains that salvation is a gift from God, and it cannot be earned by good works or personal merit.
